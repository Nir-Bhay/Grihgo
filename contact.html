Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

Once your order is confirmed, you can track it in real-time through the GrihGO app. Here's how:

  1. Open the GrihGO app
  2. Go to "My Orders" section
  3. Tap on your active order
  4. View real-time tracking with delivery partner location

You'll also receive push notifications at every stage - when the restaurant starts preparing, when the delivery partner picks up, and when they're near your location.

Our delivery charges are transparent and based on distance only. Here's our pricing:

  • 0-3 km: ‚Çπ15
  • 3-5 km: ‚Çπ25
  • 5-8 km: ‚Çπ35
  • 8+ km: ‚Çπ45

No surge pricing! We never add extra charges during peak hours or rain. The delivery fee is always shown before you place your order.

You can cancel an order easily through the app:

  1. Go to "My Orders" in the app
  2. Select the order you want to cancel
  3. Tap "Cancel Order"
  4. Select a reason and confirm

Cancellation Policy:

  • Within 2 minutes: Full refund
  • Before restaurant accepts: Full refund
  • After preparation starts: Partial or no refund

We accept all major payment methods:

  • UPI: Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM
  • Cards: Credit & Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay)
  • Wallets: Paytm, PhonePe, Amazon Pay
  • Net Banking: All major banks
  • Cash on Delivery: Pay when you receive your order

Refunds are processed automatically based on the payment method:

  • UPI/Wallets: 2-4 hours
  • Debit Card: 5-7 business days
  • Credit Card: 7-10 business days
  • Net Banking: 5-7 business days

You'll receive an email and app notification once the refund is initiated. For any delays, contact our support.

We're sorry if something went wrong! Here's how to report an issue:

  1. Go to "My Orders" and select the order
  2. Tap "Report an Issue"
  3. Select the problem (wrong item, missing item, damaged, etc.)
  4. Add photos if possible
  5. Submit - we'll respond within 30 minutes

You can also call us directly for urgent issues. We'll arrange a replacement or refund based on the situation.

Registering your restaurant is easy! Here's the process:

  1. Visit our Partner With Us page
  2. Fill out the registration form
  3. Submit required documents (FSSAI, PAN, Bank details)
  4. Our team will contact you within 24 hours
  5. Menu setup and verification (1-2 days)
  6. Go live and start receiving orders!

Bonus: 0% commission for the first 3 months!

We offer the most competitive rates in the industry:

  • First 3 months: 0% commission
  • After 3 months: 15-20% (based on order volume)
  • No hidden fees: No packaging charges, no listing fees

High-volume partners get additional discounts. Contact our business team for custom pricing.

We process payouts weekly:

  • Payout day: Every Monday
  • Settlement period: Previous Monday to Sunday
  • Processing time: 1-2 business days

You can track all your earnings and settlements in real-time through the Partner Dashboard.

You have full control over your menu through the Partner Dashboard:

  1. Log in to your Partner Dashboard
  2. Go to "Menu Management"
  3. Add, edit, or remove items
  4. Update prices and availability
  5. Changes go live immediately

Need help with menu photography? We offer free professional photoshoot for new partners!
